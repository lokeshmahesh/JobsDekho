Roles and Responsibilities - The obligations and job roles are mentioned below.
  • To ensure a positive customer experience and increase confidence in the IKEA brand, offer high-quality customer care across all remote channels (phone, chat, mail, video, and social).
  • Increase customer satisfaction, provide excellent and practical solutions while displaying a customer-centric and genuinely sympathetic approach in all interactions with customers..
  • Take ownership of the decision-making process to determine when to go above and beyond, putting the customer experience first while keeping cost sensitivity in mind
  • Employ conflict management approaches in customer contact to contribute to seamless and positive encounters that encourage customer retention
  • Gather and communicate pertinent consumer insights so that other Ingka departments may better serve the customer.
  • Participate with input to define and be accountable for achieving personal goals that are in line with and contribute to the unit, function, and team goals.
